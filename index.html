ENJOY TOURIST SPOT IN ORMOC

Lake Kasudsuran is just one of the three beautiful lakes here in Ormoc. It is located at Brgy. Gaas, Ormoc City in Leyte. The 5-hectare Lake Kasudsuran is located 27 kms. (a 45-hr. drive) northeast of Ormoc City on the virgin forest of Brgy. Ga-as and Mt. Janagdan, a plateau 1,000 m. above sea level. and is only accessible by foot.

Ormoc City is a 1st class independent component city in the province of Leyte in the region of Eastern Visayas of the Philippines.
Ormoc is the economic, cultural, commercial and transportation hub of Western Leyte.
